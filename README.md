#  Trolli HR Analytics Dashboard

## 🔍 About the Project
An interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built with **Microsoft Excel** for **Trolli Company**.  
The dashboard provides deep insights into employee data including salaries,  
demographics, departments, and hiring trends.

##  Dashboard Pages

### 📌 Report 1 – HR Overview
- **KPI Cards:** Total Employees (300), Total Salaries (1,242,807),  
  Average Experience Years
- **Count of Employees by Gender** – Donut Chart
- **Count of Employees by Extra Bonus** – Pie Chart
- **Count of Employees by Department & Work Type** – Stacked Bar Chart
- **Count of Employees by Money Transfer Method** – Bar Chart
- **Total Salaries & Employee Count by Hiring Year** – 100% Stacked Bar Chart
- **Number of Employees by Country** – Map Visual  
  *(Egypt: 99 | Saudi Arabia: 72 | Sudan: 61 | Iraq: 33 | Syria: 15)*

### 📌 Report 2 – Staff Demographics
- **Total Salaries & Employee Count by Hiring Year** – Stacked Bar Chart
- **Count of Employees by Department & Work Type** – 3D Bar Chart
- **Count of Employees by Extra Bonus** – Pie Chart
- **Count of Employees by Gender** – Donut Chart

## 🎛️ Interactive Filters
- Country Filter (Egypt, Iraq, Saudi Arabia, Sudan, Syria)
- Department Filter
- Gender Filter
- Hiring Date Slicer
- Work Type Filter
- Extra Bonus Filter

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
- Slicers & Timelines
